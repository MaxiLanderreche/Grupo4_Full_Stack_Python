AlveArt is an art gallery filled with Buenos Aires's vibes. Our main goal is to inspire people, invoke different feelings, provoke discourse and new ideas, and generally enrich the community through art.


We want to nurture our artists, promote their work, and expose them to the public, collectors, media, and cultural institutions. Furthermore, the gallery works tirelessly and strategically to advance the artists' careers and establish them in the professional art world both locally and globally.
